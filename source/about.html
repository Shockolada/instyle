About Us

Welcome dear customer

From our company’s creation, our goal has been to provide honest, high-quality, affordable cabinetry directly to homeowners and industry professionals alike. At Founder’s Choice we continually strive to bridge the gap between excellent manufacturing standards and affordability. We regularly review our processes to ensure that we are supplying our customers with a product that is not only affordable but will last for years to come.

  • 14 Years of experience
  • 359 Happy clients
  • 400 Finished projects

We are the best Interior, Exterior & Architecture Firm

Our company provides the best customer service possible from first contact to the completion of every project in order to supply you with the product and service that we would want for ourselves. Each of our four locations has a staff of passionate and experienced designers who are eager to discuss the possibilities of your project. They will work with you to exceed your expectations by listening to your thoughts, ideas and wishes to design a space that is functional and tailored to your individual taste and style. Once the design process has been completed our manufacturing team builds your cabinets and fabricates the countertops you have chosen for your space. Our talented install crews will then visit your space and install your new cabinets and countertops bringing your project to a close and supplying you with the space you’ve always dreamed about!
